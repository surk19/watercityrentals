About Water City Rentals

Chicago lakefront rentals made simple, fun, and easy to book.

Who We Are

Water City Rentals is a Chicago-based rental service offering jet ski and boat rental experiences on the lakefront. Our goal is simple: make it easier for people to enjoy Lake Michigan without complicated planning, endless phone calls, or confusing booking steps.

Whether someone wants a quick jet ski ride, a lakefront experience with friends, or a simple way to get out on the water, we focus on clear communication, easy online booking, and a smooth customer experience from start to finish.

Book your time, get your confirmation, show up, and enjoy the ride.

Our Focus

We want the rental process to feel professional, safe, and straightforward. That means clear expectations before arrival, weather-aware scheduling, proper check-in, and instructions before getting on the water.

Lake Michigan conditions can change quickly, so safety and weather conditions always come first. If conditions are not safe, bookings may need to be adjusted or rescheduled.
